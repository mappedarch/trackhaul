Section 3: Engine Fault Codes

FAULT CODE: P0300
Description: Random or Multiple Cylinder Misfire Detected
Severity: HIGH
Immediate Action Required: Yes

Symptoms:
- Engine warning light illuminated
- Rough idle or vibration
- Reduced engine power
- Increased fuel consumption

Diagnostic Steps:
1. Connect OBD-II scanner to vehicle port
2. Record all active and pending fault codes
3. Check spark plugs for wear or fouling
4. Inspect ignition coils for resistance (should be 0.5-1.5 ohms)
5. Check fuel injectors for proper operation
6. Inspect for vacuum leaks around intake manifold

Resolution:
- Replace faulty spark plugs if gap exceeds 1.1mm
- Replace ignition coil on affected cylinder
- Clean or replace fuel injectors if flow rate is below specification
- Repair vacuum leaks with approved sealant

Estimated Repair Time: 2-4 hours
Parts Required: Spark plugs (set), ignition coil (per cylinder)
Next Service Interval: 10,000 km after repair

FAULT CODE: P0171
Description: System Too Lean (Bank 1)
Severity: MEDIUM
Immediate Action Required: No — monitor for 48 hours

Symptoms:
- Reduced fuel efficiency
- Hesitation on acceleration
- Occasional rough idle

Diagnostic Steps:
1. Check mass airflow sensor readings
2. Inspect fuel pressure at rail (minimum 350 kPa)
3. Check oxygen sensor voltage (should oscillate 0.1-0.9V)
4. Inspect for air leaks after MAF sensor

Resolution:
- Clean MAF sensor with approved cleaner
- Replace fuel filter if pressure below specification
- Replace oxygen sensor if response time exceeds 100ms

Estimated Repair Time: 1-2 hours
