TRACKHAUL COMPLIANCE REFERENCE
EU Driver Hours Regulations — Regulation (EC) No 561/2006

DAILY DRIVING LIMITS
Maximum daily driving time: 9 hours
Extended daily driving time (permitted twice per week): 10 hours
Minimum daily rest period: 11 hours consecutive
Reduced daily rest (permitted 3 times between weekly rests): 9 hours consecutive

WEEKLY LIMITS
Maximum weekly driving time: 56 hours
Maximum fortnightly driving time: 90 hours
Minimum weekly rest: 45 hours consecutive
Reduced weekly rest (must be compensated within 3 weeks): 24 hours

BREAK REQUIREMENTS
After 4.5 hours of driving: mandatory break required
Minimum break duration: 45 minutes
Break may be split: 15 minutes followed by 30 minutes

TACHOGRAPH REQUIREMENTS
All vehicles over 3.5 tonnes must have a calibrated digital tachograph
Tachograph data must be downloaded every 90 days minimum
Driver card data must be downloaded every 28 days minimum
Records must be retained for 12 months minimum

PENALTIES — GERMANY
Exceeding daily driving time by up to 1 hour: EUR 30
Exceeding daily driving time by more than 2 hours: EUR 150
Missing tachograph record: EUR 250
Falsifying tachograph records: EUR 5000 and criminal prosecution

PENALTIES — NETHERLANDS
Exceeding daily driving time: EUR 100-400 depending on severity
Tachograph violations: EUR 200-800

PENALTIES — POLAND
Exceeding daily driving time: PLN 500-1500
Tachograph violations: PLN 1000-2000

AUDIT REQUIREMENTS
TrackHaul compliance team must review driver hours weekly
Automated alerts must trigger when a driver approaches 80% of any limit
Monthly compliance reports must be submitted to fleet operations director
